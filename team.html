Dr Emily Hartman

pediatrician

Dr. Emily Hartman has practiced at ABC Pediatrics since 2018. Dr. Hartman received her bachelor degree in child development from the University of Texas in Austin. She earned her medical degree from University of North Texas Health Science Center in Fort Worth. She then completed her residency at the Children’s Hospital of Oklahoma in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. Dr. Hartman is certified by the American Board of Pediatrics. She is married to her medical school sweetheart, John. They have one son and a daughter on the way. 

Dr. Hartman is accepting new patients at the Alameda location.
